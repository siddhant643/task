About Us

Verge Advisors is a global advisory firm founded in 2021 by a team with more than 70 years’ worth of collective experience. We closely work with New Age Technology companies in United States and United Kingdom and help establish them in India, Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Together, our diverse team has advised some of the biggest corporate clients of India, Middle East, and Asia. We empower organizations to grow sustainably and expand across geographical boundaries. Verge leverages its strong relationships across numerous industries to deliver cutting edge solutions for firms to build competitive advantage and expand themselves in Asia and Middle East.



At Verge, we believe in three core values – collaboration, diversity, and social impact. We work together continuously and solve challenging problems. We respect every individual’s uniqueness and embrace each other’s ideas with openness. We take pride in enabling people to not only access but also make use of technology to transform their lives.

Vinit Parikh

Vinit is a Partner at Verge Advisors. He has a vast experience of 20 years in the Finance & Investments industry. Vinit serves on the Board of Niche Financial Services Pvt Ltd. (Niche), a NBFC registered with Reserve Bank of India and Trishakti Power Pvt. Ltd., executing 250 MW thermal power plant in the state of Uttar Pradesh. He has worked with Benchmark Asset Management, an asset management company specializing in Passive Investment and Exchange Traded Fund (ETF). At Benchmark, he has worked in various aspects of investments such as compliance, investment management and portfolio management. In addition, Vinit has also served on the board of India Capital Markets Pvt. Ltd., a stock market trading platform and provided other financial services namely portfolio management, investment banking, commodity broking services and depository participate with NSDL & CDSL. There, Vinit was handling the corporate finance, institutional business, and compliance committee. Over a period of time, Vinit has also invested in start-ups in the field of Education, Food & Beverage, and Fintech. He is on the board of Eshiksa Technology Services Pvt. Ltd., which is an ed-tech start-up.
Vinit is a Charted Accountant (C.P.A) by profession.

Raghav Chaturvedi

Raghav is a Partner at Verge Advisors. With his experience in Silicon Valley, Raghav has mapped out the latest trends in the technology world and analyzed their implementation in underpenetrated countries. He has traveled extensively and worked in different parts of India to identify the most pressing issues of the society. Born and brought up in Mumbai, Raghav remains strongly committed to make technology more accessible to people and solve their problems. Alongside Verge, Raghav serves as the Director and CFO of Tijoree Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Raghav received his B.S. in Economics with a concentration in Finance from The Wharton School and B.S. in Chemical & Biomolecular Engineering from School of Engineering & Applied Science at University of Pennsylvania in 2021. He is a Moelis Fellow and will be attending his MBA at The Wharton School in 2025.

Our Mission



To foster the growth of new age technology companies in emerging markets. At Verge, it is our goal to expand the use of technology and open new avenues of growth for companies in artificial intelligence, software, internet-of-things, fintech, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals industries. In a continuously changing world, we aim to create value for new age technology companies and drive sustainable growth across diverse industries.





Our Vision



To help people access the latest technology and improve lives throughout the world. We believe technology can transform lives and provide innumerable opportunities to grow and succeed. By introducing the latest innovations in emerging markets, Verge is committed to drive technology-led growth of economies and make a positive impact on society.
